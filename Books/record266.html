Synopsis The world of Pooh is a world of enchantment.  It is a world where Winnie-the-Pooh, Christopher Robin and their friends find all sorts of adventures: from hunting Woozles and going on an expotition to the North Pole, to building a house for Eeyore and trying to unbounce Tigger.

A.A. Milne's eternally appealing characters, brought wonderfully to life by E.H. Shepard's illustrations, were originally introduced in two classic story books: <i>Winnie-the-Pooh</i> and <i>The House At Pooh Corner</i>.

This special edition, combining both books in one volume, was first published in 1958, and includes a series of full colour pictures added by E.H. Shepard to accompany his unforgettable black and white drawings.
