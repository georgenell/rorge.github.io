Synopsis Young Jim Hawkins' adventures begin when he finds the map to Captain Flint's fabulous treasure.
He sails on the <i>Hispaniola</i> with his friends Dr Livesey and Squire Trelawney, bound for Skeleton Island.  But it soon turns out that most of the crew are cut-throat pirates, led by the notorious Long John Silver, who will stop at nothing to take the treasure for themselves.
The story of how Jim tries to help the doctor and the squire outwit and out-fight Silver and his brutal companions is one of the most exciting ever written.
