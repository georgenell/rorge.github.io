Synopsis After the dramatic changes that have already taken place during the begining of the 1990s, how will our world change as the Millenium approaches?  More importantly, what can we do to survive - and prosper?

<i>The Great Reckoning</i> is a guide to rational living in irrational times.  Here, leading investment forcasters James Dale Davidson and William Rees-Mogg forsee:
- The collapse of the welfare state worldwide
- Rocketing taxes
- Further property collapse
- Decriminalization of many drugs to preserve social order
- Disintegration of many multinational countries including Canada, India, South Africa, Israel and Ethiopia
- Terrorist and small nations or break-away groups to acquire nuclear weapons
- Major migration away from big cities.

With these startling predictions backed by hundreds of investment tips and forecasts, <i>The Great Reckoning</i> provides indispensable information to meet the challenges of the final years of the 20th Century.
