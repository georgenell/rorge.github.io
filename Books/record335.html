Synopsis <i>The Plumb Line</i> leads you through the fascinating life and varied careers of Henry Plumb, from teenage farmer in the Second World War to President of the NFU, from elected MEP to President of the European Parliament.  Now in the House of Lords as Lord Plumb of Coleshill - the name of the family farm - he leads a campaign to promote food production world wide to fee the hungry by teaching better farming.

He has met the great and the grand in the world of politics and never lost the common touch.  In <i>The Plumb Line</i> he shows how his vision grew from a national to a European and finally a world perspective.  Anyone interested in food and farming and the personalities involved in Britain, in Europe and in the wider world, will be fascinated by this story.
