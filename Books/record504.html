Synopsis When did you last read yours?


For over 75 years <i>The Highway Code</i> has been the official guide to using the roads safely and legally.  It has contributes enormously to road safety and reliable road transport.
However, every day, on average around nine people are killed and around 80 seriously injured in road collisions.  So it is as important as ever that all road users, including drivers, motorcyclists, cyclists, horse riders and pedestrians, should update their knowledge of <i>The Highway Code</i>.
 - New and ameneded rules.
