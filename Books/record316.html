Synopsis <i>The Windeater/Te Kaihau</i> is Keri Hulme's first book of short stories.  Set against the beautiful dramatic natural world of her native New Zealand, she returns to the same themes explored in her novel: violence against man and nature; alienation and solitude; the usual and unusual problems which can mar human relationships.  Each story is written with a powerful, almost mystical feel for the natural world and confirms her reputation as one of the most exciting talents writing the in the English language today.
