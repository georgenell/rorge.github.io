Synopsis Completely updated in its third edition, Your pregnancy Week by Week is the top-selling pregnancy book written by a doctor.



Dr. Curtis designed its unique format to help all women from before they conceive their baby until they give birth. Read the book straight through when you first buy it, then re-read chapters as the weeks pass -- one for each week throughout your entire pregnancy. Learn how your baby is developing and review changes in your own body as they happen. In a timely way, read about lab tests you may take, how your actions affect your baby, and any possible complications you may be facing. These important facts are easily absorbed at the right time.



A vast amount of invaluable information about the entire pregnancy is included as well, a bit at a time in each chapter. And delightful new feature offers a "tip of the week" to experienced mothers as well as women pregnant for the first time.



The most up-to-date book available for pregnant women, Your Pregnancy Week by Week is written by an obstetrician with an active practice who always find time to keep on the cutting edge of medical knowledge. He addresses the concerns and questions of thousands of his patients in this award-winning book.
