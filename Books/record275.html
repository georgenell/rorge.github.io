Synopsis The history of a single parish in Argyll - but a very large parish and one so placed as to be almost an island; although specifically a history of Morvern, the author presents the material in such a way as to provide an insight into the way of life of the inhabitants of a wider area. He highlights the impact of the economic changes of the nineteenth century, including famine and emigration. The fruit of extensive research, this important and definitive work was first published in 1968 but was unobtainable for many years until this new edition was published.
