Synopsis Arranged in alphabetical form, the entries in the <i>Wordsworth Thesaurus</i> are ideally suited to hom, office, and student use and are designed to provide the word being sought quickly and easily every time.  Set in clear type and with over 150,000 entries, this accessible thesaurus will provide the user with the precise word when it is needed.

The book is generously cross-referenced, so that from whatever starting point, the <i>Wordsworth Thesaurus</i> leads the way to the right word.  This edition has been fully revised to include both British and American spellings.
