Synopsis Many people will know James Allen from ITV's Formula One commentary team. And since leaving ITV, James has continued his career of writing and journalism. Today, his website is massively popular with what one might term the more 'thoughtful' Formula One fans. This book, which is a collection of the best posts from the website this season, is all set against the backdrop of one of the most tense title battles in years, as five top drivers battled for the right to be champion. The book is supported with images by F1's leading photographer, Darren Heath. Foreword written by Mark Webber.
