Synopsis <blockquote><i>Tandia sat waiting anxiously for the fight to begin between the man she loved the most and the man she hated the most in the world.</i></blockquote>Tandia is a child of Africa: half Indian, half African, beautiful and intelligent, she is only sixteen when she is first brutalised by the police.  Her fear of the white man leads her to join the black resistance movement, where she trains as a terrorist.

With her in the fight for justice is the one white man Tandia can trust, the welterweight champion of the world, Peekay.  Now he must fight their common enemy in order to save both their lives.
