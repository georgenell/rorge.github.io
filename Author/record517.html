Synopsis <blockquote>I wrote letters to many in those days… it was rather my way of screaming from my cage.</blockquote>Charles Bukowski was a uniquely talented and prolific writer, best known for his Beat Generation writing that reflected his experience of low life urban America.  <i>Selected Letters Volume 4</i> collects together correspondence with various friends, lovers and literary contracts, providing an intimate and fascinating insight into Bukowski's mind, his emotions and his attitude towards his own creativity, right up until his death in 1994.
