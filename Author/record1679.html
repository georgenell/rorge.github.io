Synopsis Following the antics of Cartman, the fat, spoilt son of a single mum who gorges on Cheesy Poofs and Happy Tarts, Kyle, an A grade student and the butt of numerous anit-Jewish jokes, Kenny who is gruesomely killed in a freak accident in every episode, and leader Stan, who is so in love with Wendy Testaburger that he throws up on seeing her, and also owns a gay dog called Sparkie (played by George Clooney), this cartoon has cast a politically incorrect eye on modern life. This is the official South Park scriptbook and contains ten of the best South Park scripts, with Kenny, Stan, Kyle and Cartman at their dysfunctional best. From Christmas Poo to Chef's good lovin' and Mega-Streisand to Schozoid Mr garrison, this should be of interest to all South Park fans.
