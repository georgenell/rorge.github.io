Synopsis This is a wildly successful book at putting man firmly in his place.  It shows that, despite his large brain, man is still a primate - in his sexual and social life, in his affections, and in his eating habits, he is still similar to his ape ancestors.

One thing is clear from Dr. Desmon Morris's book : evolution has made man the sexiest primate alive, thus allowing him to fall in love and to stay in love long enough to rear a family.
