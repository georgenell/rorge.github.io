Synopsis In this special collector' edition, three great Asterix stories are collected in one fabulous volume for the first time ever.
In <i>Asterix and the Actress</i>, Asterix and Obelix celebrate their joint birthday party - and some surprise guests have been invited!
<i>Asterix and the Class Act</i> contains 14 stories, including tales of the day Asterix and Obelix were born (in the middle of a village fish fight).
In <i>Asterix and the Falling Sky</i>, the Gauls' one fear - that the sky may fall on their heads - looks like it might come true…
There is no better way to enjoy the antics of our indomitable hero and his friends.
