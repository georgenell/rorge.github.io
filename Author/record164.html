Synopsis If you're a guy looking for the lowdown on fifty of the most important things that really matter in life - from how to fix a dripping tap to choosing wine at a restaurant - who better to ask than your own dad?  Chances are he knows a thing or two, like how to smoke a cigar, win an arm wrestle and ask for a raise.

The trouble is, in your moment of need he's rarely at arm's length, especially when you're trying to get served in a crowded pub, or attempting to unclasp a bra with one hand at two in the morning.

When you dad's not available, check out this collection of indispensable advice.  It's like having your father in the palm of your hand (sort of) any time, anywhere.  Don't leave home without it.
