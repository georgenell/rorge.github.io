Synopsis Nigel Whittingon represents all that is honourable in the British newspaper industry and has reached the pinnicale of his career as editor of the highly respectable <i>Age</i>.  However, like most newspapers, the <i>Age</i> is owned by a corporate giant <i>Global Media Corp</i>, run by the super-bully Felix, infamous for the creative ways he sacks editors.

When Nigel is called to Felix's office and is not fired, he is enormously relieved, but that relief soon turns to horror when Felix reveals his special project, a new tabloid called <i>True Facts.</i>

In Bob Jones' hilarious bestselling novel <i>Ogg</i>, the international tourist business was the centre of his attention.  In this new black comedy, Bob turns his focus on the international newspaper industry and the destructive consequences of welfarism excesses.
