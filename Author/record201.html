Synopsis Bilbo Baggins is a hobbit who enjoys a comfortable, unambitious life, rarely travelling any further than his pantry or cellar.  But his contentment is disturbed when the wizzard Gandalf, and a company of swarves arrive on his doorstep one day to whisk him away on an adventure.  They have a plot to raid the treasure hoard guarded by Smaug the Magnificent, a large and very dangerous dragon.  Bilbo is most reluctant to take part in this quest, but he surprises even himself by his resourcefulness and his skill as a burglar!
